Setup B (Big Package) — Key Considerations
Why Setup B Makes Sense
+ 2× production capacity for ~$600K more — grain handling & bottling nearly identical in both quotes.
+ Lower fully-loaded cost per bottle — same fixed overhead spread over 2× the bottles.
+ Contract distilling ready — excess capacity can generate revenue from Day 1 while bourbon ages.
+ More jobs created — aligns with city partnership and community investment goals.
+ Growth headroom for 7+ years. Setup A could max out by year 3–4.
+ Setup B at 40% utilization = Setup A at 80% utilization in bottle output.
Things to Plan For
− Higher startup cost (~$612K more) — requires more capital or larger financing package.
− Setup B needs ~70% sell-through to match Setup A's payback at 100% sell-through.
− More bourbon aging capital tied up — 2× production = 2× barrels in storage for 3+ years.

1. Production Assumptions

Still sizes, run frequency, product mix. Change these to model different operating scenarios.

2. Yields & Pricing

Finished gallons per gallon of still capacity, wholesale & tasting room pricing, bourbon aging split.

3. Sell-Through Ramp — Most Important Input

What % of production you actually sell each year. Default ramps from 20% to 75% over 5 years. This drives everything downstream. Adjust on the ROI tab using the sliders.

4. Startup Costs

Equipment + installation from the Equipment tab, plus a placeholder for other costs (licensing, working capital, contingency).

8. Cost Per Bottle — Key Section

Variable cost per bottle is identical for both setups. The fully-loaded cost (including fixed overhead) is lower for Setup B because the same overhead is spread over 2× the bottles.

9. Year-by-Year P&L

Years 1–3 are pre-aged-bourbon. Year 4 is when bourbon revenue kicks in — you'll see a significant jump. Cumulative profit tracks payback progress. Interactive on the ROI tab.

10. Crossover Analysis

Setup B pays back faster at ≥60% sell-through — even with its higher startup cost. At 60% (average craft distillery), Setup B pays back in 8 months vs. 16 for Setup A.

11. Contract Distilling

Not included in the P&L. Standalone calculator for Setup B's unused capacity. At conservative estimates ($8/PG, 2 runs/wk), adds ~$576K/year in revenue.

What This Model Does Not Include
• Financing / debt service (pure cash ROI)
• Contract distilling in the P&L
