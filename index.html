The Case For Setup B
+ 2× capacity for ~$579K more — grain handling & bottling nearly identical in both quotes.
+ Lower fully-loaded cost per bottle — same fixed overhead spread over 2× the bottles.
+ Contract distilling ready — excess capacity earns revenue while bourbon ages.
+ More jobs created — aligns with city partnership goals.
+ 7+ years of growth headroom. Setup A could max out by Year 3–4.
+ Setup B at 40% utilization = Setup A at 80% output.
Things to Plan For
− Higher startup (~$579K more) — more capital or a larger financing package required.
− Needs ~70% sell-through to match Setup A payback at 100%.
− 2× bourbon aging capital tied up — 2× barrels in storage for 3+ years.
